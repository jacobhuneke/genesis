# genesis

## Introduction
In this project I attempt to gather more meaning from the book of Genesis by comparing the etymology of the words in the KJV with the definitions of the latin, greek, and aramaic words in the same verse. I believe through the multiple translations and versions of the Bible, meaning may have been lost with some words as historians and scholars attempted to match the best word at their disposal to the existing one in the previous translation. Through etymology and the definitions of the other words, I will attempt to write phrases to substitute with the words of the english versions to offer greater understanding. If successful, I will move on to doing this with other books of the Bible.

## Sources
